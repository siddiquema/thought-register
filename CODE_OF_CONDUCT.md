# Code of Conduct

Be respectful. Be constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Disagree about ideas, not people.
- Give feedback that helps the project, not feedback that just wins an argument.
- Assume good intent, and give others the benefit of the doubt.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory remarks.
- Deliberately derailing discussions or issues.
- Bad-faith contributions intended to waste maintainers' time.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported privately to **siddiquema@gmail.com**. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
