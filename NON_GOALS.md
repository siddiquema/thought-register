# Non-Goals

Thought Register has one job: capture knowledge before it disappears. Everything else is secondary, and some things are explicitly out of bounds.

## What this project will never become

Regardless of how the product evolves, Thought Register will not turn into:

- Task Manager
- Calendar
- Project Management Tool
- Wiki
- CMS
- AI Chatbot
- CRM
- Social Platform
- Knowledge Graph Platform

These are not missing features. They are different products, solving different problems.

## What this project is not competing with

Thought Register is not trying to replace or rival:

- Notion
- Obsidian
- Evernote
- OneNote
- Google Keep
- Roam Research

Those tools optimize for organizing and connecting knowledge once it exists. Thought Register optimizes for the moment before that — getting the thought out of someone's head before it's lost.

## Non-goal vs. deferred

A non-goal listed here is permanent: it does not belong in this project's identity, regardless of demand.

A deferred idea — like the items listed under "Explicitly not on this roadmap" in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md) — may be genuinely useful and revisited later, but is intentionally excluded from the current scope until explicitly requested and evaluated against [MANIFESTO.md](MANIFESTO.md).

When in doubt about which category something falls into, raise it before building it.
